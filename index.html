Hello there!

I am a Ph.D. student in Human-Computer Interaction at the Games Engineering Group, University of Würzburg. My research interests span across extended reality and user experience design. 

I served as a software engineer at AMMACHI Labs, Amrita University where much of my work was in virtual reality and serious games, haptics, social robotics, and computational thinking.
